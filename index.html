About

NUCC is an adventurous caving club that travels to anywhere with a pitch, pot or pub. If you are in search of an adventure you will see caverns carved out by the power of water and geological marvels hidden underground. If you are interested we will train you up, kit you up and send you underground.

Why caving

If you want to explore huge caverns, subterranean stream, abandoned mines or abseil vast shafts then this club is for you. If you want a dark place to hide in look no further.

Can I cave

Most people can. You don’t have to be very fit and you don’t need any experience. We will get you cave ready.

How to join

If you are currently doing your degree you are welcome.

To join do one of these three steps

Come to our stall at the fresher’s fair.

Come to one of our fresher’s events.

If you are not a University of Nottingham student, you will have to be an associate member.
